NGO HUB

Connect. Collaborate. Impact.

NGO HUB is your central platform for partner organizations and campaign dashboards. Sign in to access portals, track budgets, and manage initiatives.

How to use

  1. Sign in using your email (any valid email works).
  2. Select a partner NGO from the home list.
  3. Enter the partner access password — hint: company ID + 123.
  4. Explore campaigns, budgets, and events.
  • Secure single sign-on
  • Multiple partner portals
  • Campaign budget tracking
